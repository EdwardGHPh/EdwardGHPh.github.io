Hello! I'm Edward, an MSc Physics student at the University of Ottawa, working under the supervision of Professor Stefanie Czischek in Canada. I am originally from Colombia, where I completed my undergraduate studies at Universidad del Quindío and collaborated with Professor Édgar Arturo Gómez González in the Theoretical & Computational Physics Group (GFTC). My primary research interests include quantum mechanics, particularly quantum computing and information, semi-classical physics, and many-body condensed matter systems.

When I'm not immersed in physics, I enjoy learning new languages, café hopping, bar hopping, and swimming! Welcome to my personal page—here, you'll find everything from my resume (for professional and academic purposes) to photos of birds and more.
